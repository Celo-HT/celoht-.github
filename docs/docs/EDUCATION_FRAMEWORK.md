# CeloHT Education Framework

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

The CeloHT Education Framework defines the structure and principles used to design educational programs focused on financial literacy, digital inclusion, blockchain awareness, and community empowerment.

The framework supports accessible learning for individuals with different levels of technical experience.

---

# Education Mission

The mission of CeloHT education is to help communities understand and use digital tools responsibly while developing financial knowledge and practical skills.

---

# Learning Principles

CeloHT education follows:

- Accessibility
- Practical learning
- Community participation
- Inclusion
- Transparency
- Continuous improvement

---

# Learning Levels

## Level 1 — Digital Foundations

Purpose:

Introduce basic digital concepts.

Topics:

- Internet basics
- Digital security
- Online accounts
- Digital identity
- Safe technology use

---

## Level 2 — Financial Literacy

Purpose:

Develop better understanding of personal and community finance.

Topics:

- Saving
- Budgeting
- Payments
- Financial responsibility
- Economic opportunities

---

## Level 3 — Blockchain & Web3 Education

Purpose:

Introduce blockchain technology in a simple and responsible way.

Topics:

- What is blockchain
- Wallets
- Transactions
- Stablecoins
- Decentralized systems
- Security practices

---

## Level 4 — Celo Ecosystem Education

Purpose:

Explain Celo-related tools and concepts.

Topics:

- Celo Blockchain
- cUSD
- CELO
- Valora
- Community applications

---

## Level 5 — Community Leadership

Purpose:

Develop local educators and community contributors.

Topics:

- Teaching skills
- Event organization
- Community building
- Leadership principles
- Open-source collaboration
