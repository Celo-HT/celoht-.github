# CeloHT Programs

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

CeloHT develops community programs focused on financial education, digital inclusion, environmental sustainability, and open-source collaboration.

These programs are designed to create practical impact while supporting community participation and long-term development.

---

# Program Areas

CeloHT programs are organized around four main pillars:

- Education
- Agent Network
- Reforestation
- Technology & Open Source

---

# Education Program

## Purpose

The Education Program helps communities understand digital finance, blockchain technology, and responsible use of digital tools.

## Activities

- Financial literacy training
- Web3 education
- Blockchain awareness workshops
- Digital wallet education
- Community learning resources

## Goals

- Improve financial knowledge.
- Increase digital confidence.
- Create local educators.
- Expand access to learning opportunities.

---

# Agent Network Program

## Purpose

The Agent Network supports community access to digital financial services through trusted local participants.

## Activities

- Community onboarding
- Wallet assistance
- Digital payment support
- Local guidance
- User education

## Goals

- Increase access to digital tools.
- Support underserved communities.
- Build trusted local networks.

---

# Reforestation Program

## Purpose

The Reforestation Program promotes environmental restoration and community participation.

## Activities

- Tree planting initiatives
- Environmental education
- Sustainability campaigns
- Community involvement

## Goals

- Support environmental protection.
- Increase awareness of climate challenges.
- Encourage local action.

# Technology & Open Source Program
