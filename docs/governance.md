# CeloHT Governance

## Community-Governed Initiative

CeloHT is a community-governed Web3 initiative built on transparency, collaboration, and shared responsibility.

The project was founded by **Johnny Dubic**, but CeloHT is not controlled by a single individual.

The future direction of the ecosystem is shaped through community participation, open discussion, contributions, and collective decision-making.

---

# Governance Principles

CeloHT follows these core governance principles:

- Community Participation
- Transparency
- Accountability
- Open Collaboration
- Responsible Leadership
- Inclusive Decision-Making
- Long-Term Sustainability
- Ethical Innovation

These principles guide the development of the ecosystem and its initiatives.

---

# Community Role

The CeloHT community participates by:

- Sharing ideas
- Proposing initiatives
- Reviewing projects
- Providing feedback
- Contributing resources
- Supporting programs
- Helping improve documentation

Every contributor plays a role in strengthening the ecosystem.

---

# Decision-Making

CeloHT promotes open and collaborative decision-making.

Important discussions may involve:

- Community Feedback
- Contributor Input
- Program Evaluation
- Technical Discussions
- Strategic Planning

Decisions are guided by the mission, values, and long-term goals of CeloHT.

---

# Founder Role

The founder provides initial vision, coordination, and support for the development of CeloHT.

The founder's role includes:

- Protecting the original mission
- Supporting community growth
- Encouraging collaboration
- Helping coordinate initiatives

However, the ecosystem remains community-driven.

---

# Transparency

CeloHT promotes transparency through:

- Open Documentation
- Public GitHub Repositories
- Clear Project Information
- Community Discussions
- Open Source Development

Transparency helps build trust between contributors, users, and partners.

---

# Future Governance Development

As CeloHT grows, governance structures may evolve to include:

- Community Committees
- Working Groups
- Contributor Roles
- Program Coordinators
- Community Voting Systems

The objective is to create a sustainable governance model that reflects the needs of the community.

---

# Governance Statement

**CeloHT is founded by an individual but built by a community. Its direction is shaped through collaboration, transparency, and shared responsibility.**
