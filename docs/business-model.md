# CeloHT Business Model

## Overview

CeloHT follows a community-focused and sustainable ecosystem model designed to create long-term value through Web3 education, financial inclusion, environmental initiatives, and digital innovation.

The model focuses on building practical solutions that benefit communities while maintaining transparency, open collaboration, and responsible development.

---

# Business Model Philosophy

CeloHT is not built around speculation or short-term financial activities.

Our approach focuses on:

- Community Impact
- Sustainable Growth
- Education
- Digital Inclusion
- Innovation
- Partnerships
- Real-World Adoption

---

# Core Value Creation

CeloHT creates value through three main pillars:

## Education

Creating knowledge and opportunities through:

- Web3 Training
- Blockchain Education

- Community Workshops

## Agent Network

## Reforestation

Creating environmental impact through:

# Ecosystem Participants

The CeloHT ecosystem connects:
