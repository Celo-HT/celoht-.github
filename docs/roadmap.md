# CeloHT Roadmap

## Overview

The CeloHT roadmap represents our long-term vision for building a community-driven Web3 ecosystem focused on education, financial inclusion, agent networks, and environmental sustainability.

The roadmap evolves through community collaboration, feedback, and ecosystem growth.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

## Building the Base

**Objectives:**

- Establish the CeloHT community
- Create the organizational structure
- Launch open-source repositories
- Develop project documentation
- Introduce Web3 education programs
- Promote Celo ecosystem awareness
- Educate communities about cUSD, CELO, and Valora

**Focus Areas:**

- Education
- Documentation
- Community Building
- Open Source Development

---

# Phase 2 — Community Expansion

## Growing Access and Participation

**Objectives:**

- Increase financial literacy programs
- Support local entrepreneurs

- Community Programs

# Long-Term Vision

CeloHT aims to become a recognized community-driven Web3 ecosystem that connects:

- Education
- Digital Finance
- Entrepreneurship
- Environmental Action
- Open Innovation

Our long-term goals include:

- Increasing blockchain adoption
- Supporting underserved communities
- Creating sustainable opportunities
- Developing impactful technology solutions
- Building a strong contributor network

---

# Roadmap Principles

CeloHT follows these principles while growing:

- Community First
- Transparency
- Sustainable Development
- Open Collaboration
- Responsible Innovation
- Continuous Improvement

# Community Evolution

The roadmap is not fixed.

As the ecosystem grows, new priorities and initiatives may be created through community discussion and participation.

CeloHT continues to evolve with the needs of its community.
