# About CeloHT

CeloHT is a community-governed open-source initiative focused on expanding financial inclusion, Web3 education, environmental sustainability, and community empowerment through the Celo ecosystem.

The initiative supports education, local agent networks, and reforestation while promoting accessible digital financial tools.

---

# Mission
