# CeloHT Education Program

## Education Mission

Education is one of the core pillars of CeloHT.

Our mission is to make blockchain technology, digital finance, and Web3 concepts simple, accessible, and useful for individuals and communities.

We believe knowledge is the foundation of adoption and empowerment.

---

# Education Objectives

The CeloHT Education Program focuses on:

- Increasing blockchain awareness
- Improving financial literacy
- Teaching digital payment tools
- Supporting community learning
- Developing digital skills
- Preparing people for the Web3 economy

---

# Web3 Education

CeloHT provides educational resources covering:

- Blockchain Fundamentals
- Web3 Concepts
- Decentralization
- Digital Assets
- Smart Contracts
- Wallet Security
- Decentralized Applications (dApps)

Our goal is to help beginners understand Web3 without unnecessary complexity.

---

# Financial Literacy

Financial education is essential for responsible technology adoption.

CeloHT teaches:

- Digital Finance Basics
- Saving Principles
- Payment Methods
- Stablecoins
- Transaction Awareness
- Responsible Digital Asset Management

We promote education before adoption.

---

# Celo Ecosystem Education

CeloHT helps communities understand and use tools from the Celo ecosystem.

Educational topics include:

## cUSD

- Understanding stable digital payments
- Sending and receiving transactions
- Everyday payment use cases

## CELO

- Understanding the Celo network
- Transaction fees
- Ecosystem participation

## Valora Wallet

- Wallet creation
- Secure usage
- Sending and receiving digital payments
- Basic wallet security
