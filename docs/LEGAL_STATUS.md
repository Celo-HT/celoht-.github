# Legal Status

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

CeloHT is a community-governed open-source initiative dedicated to financial education, digital inclusion, environmental sustainability, and community development.

This document explains the legal status of the project and provides important information for contributors, partners, sponsors, and community members.

---

# Organizational Status

CeloHT operates as a community initiative.

The project is governed through transparent community participation as described in GOVERNANCE.md.

---

# Legal Entity

Unless officially announced otherwise, CeloHT should not be assumed to be:

- A corporation
- A bank
- A financial institution
- A licensed investment advisor
- A regulated cryptocurrency exchange

If a legal entity is established in the future, official documentation will be published through the project's repositories and communication channels.

---

# Community Governance

Major decisions are made through the community governance process.

No single individual owns or controls the governance of CeloHT.

---

# Open Source

Software and documentation published by CeloHT are released under the licenses specified in each repository.

Contributors retain the rights granted by those licenses.

---

# Financial Activities

CeloHT may receive grants, donations, sponsorships, or other forms of community support consistent with the Funding Policy.

Receiving financial support does not alter the community-governed nature of the project.

---

# Compliance

CeloHT aims to operate in accordance with applicable laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where its activities take place.

Community members remain responsible for complying with the laws applicable to them.

---

# Intellectual Property

Project names, logos, trademarks, documentation, and other protected assets remain subject to their applicable licenses and policies.

Use of CeloHT branding should follow BRAND_GUIDELINES.md.

---

# Partnerships

Collaborations with organizations, educational institutions, companies, and nonprofits do not create ownership rights over CeloHT unless explicitly stated in a written agreement.

---

# Changes

This document may be updated as the legal status of CeloHT evolves.

Official announcements regarding legal changes will be published through the project's official communication channels.

---

# Contact

Questions regarding the legal status of CeloHT may be directed through the official contact information provided in the repository or on the official website.
