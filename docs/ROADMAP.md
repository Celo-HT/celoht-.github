# CeloHT Roadmap

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

This roadmap describes the long-term direction and development priorities of CeloHT.

CeloHT is a community-governed open-source initiative focused on financial education, digital inclusion, environmental sustainability, and community empowerment.

The roadmap evolves through community participation and changing opportunities.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

## Completed / In Progress

### Community Development

- Establish CeloHT community structure.
- Create open-source organization on GitHub.
- Publish public documentation.
- Develop governance framework.
- Build community communication channels.

### Education

- Create financial education resources.
- Introduce communities to digital finance.
- Provide Web3 and blockchain awareness training.

### Technology

- Establish technical documentation.
- Create development standards.
- Improve open-source collaboration.
