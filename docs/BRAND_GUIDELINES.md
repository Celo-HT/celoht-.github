# CeloHT Brand Guidelines

## Overview

These guidelines define how the CeloHT brand should be presented across all platforms to ensure consistency, recognition, and professionalism.

---

# Brand Name

Official name:

**CeloHT**

Always write the name exactly as shown above.

Do not use alternative spellings or abbreviations unless officially approved.

---

# Brand Mission

CeloHT empowers communities through education, digital finance, environmental sustainability, and open-source collaboration.

---

# Brand Values

- Transparency
- Community Governance
- Inclusion
- Innovation
- Sustainability
- Accessibility
- Collaboration

---

# Logo

Always use the official CeloHT logo.

The logo must:

- Maintain its original proportions.
- Remain clear and readable.
- Be displayed with adequate spacing.
- Use official colors.

Do not:

- Stretch the logo.
- Rotate the logo.
- Change the logo colors.
- Add effects or shadows.
- Place the logo on backgrounds that reduce visibility.

---

# Color Palette

Primary colors:

- Dark Blue
- Yellow

Secondary colors may be introduced for specific products while remaining consistent with the CeloHT identity.

---

# Typography

Use clean, modern, and accessible fonts.

Examples:

- Inter
- Roboto
- Open Sans

Maintain consistent typography across websites, documentation, presentations, and social media.

---

# Icons

Use simple, modern icons that align with the project's visual identity.

Avoid decorative or inconsistent icon styles.

---

# Images

Preferred imagery includes:

- Community activities
- Education
- Technology
- Nature
- Reforestation
- Collaboration

Images should be authentic, inclusive, and respectful.

---

# Tone of Voice

Communications should be:

- Clear
- Professional
- Friendly
- Inclusive
- Respectful
- Educational

Avoid misleading or exaggerated claims.

---

# Social Media

Official announcements should:

- Reflect the organization's mission.
- Use consistent branding.
- Link to official resources when appropriate.

---

# Documentation

All public documentation should:

- Follow consistent formatting.
- Use clear headings.
- Maintain professional language.
- Be regularly reviewed.

---

# Presentations

Presentation materials should:

- Use official branding.
- Follow the approved color palette.
- Include the official logo.
- Maintain a clean and readable layout.

---

# Merchandise

When producing branded materials:

- Use official logos.
- Follow color specifications.
- Preserve brand consistency.

---

# Community Projects

Community-created materials should respect these guidelines while clearly identifying unofficial content when applicable.

---

# Questions

For questions regarding brand usage, contact the CeloHT community through the official communication channels.

---

# Updates

These guidelines may be updated through the community governance process as the project evolves.
