# CeloHT Ambassador Program

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

The CeloHT Ambassador Program empowers community members to represent CeloHT in their local communities while promoting financial education, digital inclusion, environmental sustainability, and open-source collaboration.

Ambassadors serve as trusted community leaders who help expand the reach and impact of CeloHT.

---

# Mission

The Ambassador Program exists to:

- Expand community education.
- Promote responsible blockchain adoption.
- Support financial inclusion.
- Strengthen local communities.
- Encourage open-source participation.
- Advance environmental initiatives.

---

# Ambassador Responsibilities

Ambassadors are encouraged to:

- Organize educational events.
- Introduce new community members.
- Support local workshops.
- Promote CeloHT initiatives.
- Encourage GitHub contributions.
- Help answer community questions.
- Build partnerships with local organizations.
- Represent CeloHT professionally.

---

# Eligibility

Applicants should:

- Support the mission and values of CeloHT.
- Demonstrate integrity and professionalism.
- Respect community governance.
- Follow the Code of Conduct.
- Be willing to learn and collaborate.

Prior blockchain experience is helpful but not required.

---

# Areas of Contribution

Ambassadors may focus on one or more areas:

- Education
- Community Development
- Agent Network
- Environmental Sustainability
- Open Source
- Partnerships
- Communications

---

# Training

Selected ambassadors may receive training on:

- CeloHT programs
- Community leadership
- Public speaking
- Blockchain fundamentals
- Financial education
- GitHub collaboration
- Event organization

---

# Code of Conduct

Ambassadors must always:

- Treat everyone with respect.
- Communicate honestly.
- Avoid discrimination or harassment.
- Protect the reputation of CeloHT.
- Follow community governance decisions.

Violations may result in suspension or removal from the program.

---

# Recognition

Active ambassadors may receive:

- Public recognition
- Digital certificates
- Leadership opportunities
- Invitations to community initiatives
- Featured profiles in community reports

Recognition is based on meaningful contributions rather than seniority.

---

# Performance Review

Ambassador participation may be reviewed periodically based on:

- Community engagement
- Educational activities
- Collaboration
- Professional conduct
- Contributions to CeloHT

The purpose of reviews is to support growth and maintain program quality.

---

# Equal Opportunity

The Ambassador Program is open to individuals from all countries and backgrounds.

CeloHT is committed to building an inclusive, respectful, and diverse global community.

---

# How to Apply

Interested community members may:

1. Join the CeloHT community.
2. Participate in discussions and activities.
3. Contribute to projects or events.
4. Submit an application when ambassador opportunities are announced.

Selection is based on demonstrated commitment, community participation, and alignment with CeloHT's values.

---

# Community Governance

The Ambassador Program operates under CeloHT's community governance model.

Appointments, updates, and program evolution are guided through transparent community processes rather than individual authority.

---

# Contact

Questions about the Ambassador Program may be directed through the official CeloHT communication channels listed in the repository or on the official website.
