# CeloHT Impact

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

CeloHT is a community-governed open-source initiative that promotes financial inclusion, Web3 education, environmental sustainability, and community empowerment through the Celo ecosystem.

This document summarizes the measurable impact of the initiative and will continue to evolve as new programs and data become available.

---

# Areas of Impact

CeloHT focuses on five core areas:

- Financial Education
- Digital Inclusion
- Agent Network
- Environmental Sustainability
- Open Source Development

---

# Financial Education

The education program helps individuals understand:

- Personal finance
- Digital payments
- Stablecoins
- Blockchain fundamentals
- Self-custody
- Digital wallets
- Responsible use of Web3 technologies

Educational activities include:

- Community workshops
- Training sessions
- Online learning resources
- Public presentations

---

# Digital Inclusion

CeloHT works to reduce barriers to digital financial services by supporting:

- Wallet onboarding
- Digital payment literacy
- Access to stablecoins
- Community support
- Practical blockchain education

---

# Agent Network

The community Agent Network contributes by providing:

- Wallet assistance
- Cash-in services
- Cash-out services
- Payment support
- Community education
- Local guidance

The network is designed to expand access to digital financial tools in underserved communities.
