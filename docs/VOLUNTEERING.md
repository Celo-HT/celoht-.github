# Volunteering

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

Volunteers are an essential part of the CeloHT community.

People from all backgrounds are welcome to contribute their time, knowledge, skills, and experience to help advance CeloHT's mission.

---

# Who Can Volunteer?

Anyone who shares the values of CeloHT may participate, including:

- Students
- Educators
- Developers
- Designers
- Translators
- Community Leaders
- Environmental Advocates
- Researchers
- Content Creators
- Blockchain Enthusiasts

No previous experience with blockchain is required for many volunteer opportunities.

---

# Volunteer Areas

Volunteers may contribute in one or more areas:

## Education

- Organize workshops

- Support community learning
- Translate educational materials

# Volunteer Principles

All volunteers are expected to:

- Act respectfully.
- Support the community.
- Follow the Code of Conduct.
- Protect community members.
- Respect privacy.
- Promote inclusion.
- Collaborate openly.

---

# Time Commitment

Volunteers may contribute according to their availability.

There is no minimum number of required hours.

Every contribution is valuable.

---

# Recognition

CeloHT values every volunteer contribution.

Recognition may include:

- Contributor acknowledgements
- Community appreciation
- Digital certificates (when applicable)
- Public recognition
- Leadership opportunities

Recognition is based on participation and community contribution.

---

# Training

Training opportunities may include:

- Community onboarding
- Blockchain fundamentals
- Financial education
- Environmental programs
- GitHub collaboration
- Leadership development

---

# Code of Conduct

All volunteers must follow:

- CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md
- GOVERNANCE.md
- CONTRIBUTING.md

Failure to follow community standards may result in suspension or removal from volunteer activities.

---

# Equal Opportunity

CeloHT welcomes volunteers regardless of nationality, gender, language, ethnicity, disability, religion, or professional background.

The community is committed to providing an inclusive and respectful environment for everyone.

---

# How to Join

Individuals interested in volunteering may:

- Join the CeloHT community.
- Participate in GitHub Discussions.
- Contribute to repositories.
- Attend community events.
- Contact the community through official communication channels.

---

# Community First

Volunteering at CeloHT is built on collaboration, transparency, and service.

Every contribution—large or small—helps strengthen the community and advance the mission of expanding education, digital inclusion, and environmental sustainability.
