# About CeloHT

CeloHT is a community-driven Web3 initiative focused on financial inclusion, digital education, environmental sustainability, and community development.

Built on the Celo ecosystem, CeloHT creates practical solutions that connect communities with accessible blockchain technology.

The project focuses on real-world adoption through:

- Education
- Digital Payments
- Agent Networks
- Entrepreneurship
- Environmental Action
- Open Collaboration

CeloHT was founded by **Johnny Dubic** and is governed through community participation, collaboration, and shared decision-making.

---

Our mission is to make blockchain technology accessible and useful by empowering communities through:
