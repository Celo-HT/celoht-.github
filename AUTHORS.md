Founder

Johnny Dubic

Johnny Dubic founded CeloHT with the vision of expanding Web3 education, financial inclusion, community development, and environmental sustainability across Haiti and the Caribbean.

While Johnny Dubic initiated the project, CeloHT is community-governed. Strategic decisions, ecosystem development, and community initiatives are shaped through open collaboration, discussion, and community participation rather than by a single individual.
